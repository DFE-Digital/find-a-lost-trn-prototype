Changes to an official name will update their name in:

  • Get an identity
  • Database of Qualified Teachers (DQT)

Verify this name change

You must verify this name change with an official document confirming their new name.

You need the following documents:

  • For marriage: a copy of their marriage certificate
  • For a civil partnership: a copy of their civil agreement certificate
  • For divorce: a copy of their decree absolute or birth certificate
  • For a statutory declaration: a copy of the statutory declaration
  • For deed poll: a copy of their deed poll

To fix a typo, a copy of their passport is enough.
