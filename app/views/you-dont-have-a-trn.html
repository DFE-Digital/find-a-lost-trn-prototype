{% extends "layouts/default.html" %} {% set title = "If you do not have a TRN"%} {% block pageNavigation %} {{ govukBackLink({ href: paths.back }) }} {% endblock %} {% set text %} ## What a TRN is for You need a TRN to: * [access your teaching qualifications](https://access-your-teaching-qualifications.education.gov.uk/qualifications/sign-in) to download your certificates, change the name on them or check details of your initial teacher training and induction * give to employers so they can complete mandatory teacher status checks * manage records of your contributions to the Teachers’ Pensions scheme * give to the Department for Education (DfE) when you have been awarded QTS so you can begin your early career teacher induction * be a [mentor for a trainee or early career teacher](https://www.gov.uk/guidance/teacher-reference-number-trn#if-youre-a-mentor-for-a-trainee-or-early-career-teacher) * [register for an NPQ](https://register-national-professional-qualifications.education.gov.uk/registration_closed)
! Warning You only need a TRN if one of the above applies to you. You will not get one for any other reason.
