{% extends "layouts/default.html" %} {% block pageNavigation %} {{ govukBackLink({ href: paths.back }) }} {% endblock %} {% set title = "You do not have a TRN"%} {% set text %} ## What a teacher reference number (TRN) is for You need a TRN to: * view your record with the Teaching Regulation Agency (TRA), and download professional certificates in the [Teacher self service portal](https://teacherservices.education.gov.uk/SelfService/Login?_ga=2.206960218.899524735.1637088337-1609244267.1609598427) * give to employers so they can complete mandatory teacher status checks * manage records of your contributions to the Teachers’ Pensions scheme * give to the Department for Education (DfE) when you have been awarded QTS so you can begin your early career teacher induction * register for a national professional qualification (NPQ) ## Get a TRN to register for an NPQ You do not need to be a teacher to get a TRN. ### Request a TRN by email If you’ve never been given a TRN, email the Teaching Regulation Agency at [qts.enquiries@education.gov.uk](mailto:qts.enquiries@education.gov.uk). The team aim to respond to valid email requests within 5 working days. You’ll need to provide contact details and proof of ID. Use the free Galaxkey secure email platform to ensure your identification documents are protected. ### Register to send a secure email To send a secure email to the department, go to the [Galaxkey site](https://manager.galaxkey.com/services/registerme). Enter the email address that you wish to sign up with and complete the registration process. You’ll be sent an email to activate your account once you have completed this step. You can then send secure emails to the Teaching Regulation Agency using Galaxkey. ### Send your ID documents securely Follow these steps to send your documents through Galaxkey: 1. Select ‘Compose’ to create a new secure email. 2. Enter ‘qts.enquiries@education.gov.uk’ as the recipient. 3. Enter ‘Undertaking an NPQ, do not have a TRN and require a number to be issued’ as the subject line. 4. Include the following information in your email: * your full legal name * your date of birth * an email address for the team to reply to 5. Attach a scanned copy or photo of one of the following types of ID: * passport * driving licence (full or provisional) * certificate of residence * birth certificate Follow the guidance on screen to make sure you send the correct file size. Find out more about how the Teaching Regulation Agency processes your data on the [teacher self-service site](https://www.gov.uk/guidance/teacher-self-service-portal). ### What happens next The Teaching Regulation Agency will use the information you send to create a permanent record and your TRN. {% endset %} {% block content %}
